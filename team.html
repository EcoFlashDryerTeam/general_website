Why We Started Eco Flash Dryer

A team united by a shared vision to solve Uganda's cassava crisis

The Problem We Witnessed

Cassava is Uganda's second-most important food crop, feeding millions and supporting countless farming families. But we saw a devastating pattern: farmers would harvest their cassava, only to watch 30-40% of it spoil within days. Traditional sun-drying took too long, depended on good weather, and often resulted in moldy, low-quality product that fetched poor prices.

We met farmers who worked tirelessly but still struggled financially. We saw entire harvests lost to rain during the drying season. We realized that the technology existed to solve this—it just needed to be adapted for Ugandan farmers: affordable, solar-powered, and practical.

Our team came together with diverse expertise—engineering, nutrition, social science, and agriculture—united by one goal: create a flash drying solution that actually works for smallholder farmers. The result is Eco Flash Dryer: fast, affordable, sustainable, and designed specifically for Uganda's cassava farmers.
