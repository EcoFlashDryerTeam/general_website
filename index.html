Why Flash Drying Matters

Addressing Uganda's critical cassava post-harvest challenge

The Problem

Uganda produces 3.5M tonnes of cassava annually, but farmers lose 30-40% to spoilage within 2-3 days of harvest. Traditional sun-drying takes 3-5 days and depends on weather, leading to mold, quality loss, and financial hardship.

Flash Drying Solution

Our technology rapidly removes moisture in 6-8 hours using controlled heat from solar panels and biomass fuel. This "flash" process preserves nutrients, prevents spoilage, and gives farmers year-round drying capability regardless of weather.

The Impact

Farmers reduce losses by 45%, dry cassava 80% faster, cut energy costs by 60%, and access premium markets. Better quality dried cassava means higher income and improved food security for farming communities.

Flash Drying in 3 Steps

Simple process, powerful results

1

Load Fresh Cassava

Harvest and peel cassava. Place chips in drying trays. Cassava peels are collected for biomass fuel.

2

Flash Dry with Heat

Solar panels or biomass heater generate controlled heat. Hot air circulates rapidly, removing moisture in 6-8 hours.

3

Harvest Premium Product

Collect dried cassava with preserved nutrients and color. Store for months or sell immediately at premium prices.
