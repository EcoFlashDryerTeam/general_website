How to Get Started

Simple 4-step process from inquiry to drying your first batch

1

Contact Us for Consultation

Call, email, or WhatsApp our team. We'll discuss your cassava production volume, location, and which acquisition model suits your needs. Free consultation, no obligation.

2

Site Visit & Demo (Optional)

We can arrange a site visit to your farm or invite you to our demo facility in Kampala. See the dryer in action, ask questions, and verify it meets your needs before committing.

3

Finalize Agreement & Installation

Choose your acquisition model (purchase, lease, or pay-per-use). For purchases/leases, we deliver and install the dryer at your farm within 2-4 weeks. Professional installation included.

4

Training & Start Drying

Our technician provides hands-on training (2-3 hours) covering loading, operation, monitoring, and maintenance. You'll dry your first batch with expert guidance. Ongoing support available via phone/WhatsApp.

Questions Before You Contact Us?

Quick answers to help you make an informed decision

Do you offer delivery across Uganda?

Yes! We deliver and install throughout Uganda. Delivery costs vary by location and are calculated during your consultation. Remote areas may require additional logistics coordination.

What warranty and support do you provide?

All dryers include a 5-year warranty on major components (solar panels, heating system, structural frame). We provide phone/WhatsApp support, spare parts availability, and annual maintenance visits (first year included).

Can cooperatives or farmer groups apply?

Absolutely! We offer special cooperative packages and volume discounts. Many of our clients are farmer cooperatives sharing one or more dryers. This model works particularly well with our lease-to-own option.

What if I have questions after installation?

Our support team is available via phone and WhatsApp during business hours. We also provide an operations manual, troubleshooting videos, and can arrange technician visits if needed. You're never on your own.
