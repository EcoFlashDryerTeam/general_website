From Research to Reality

How we developed the Eco Flash Dryer with farmers at the center

1

Farmer Interviews & Field Research

We spent 6 months in cassava-growing regions, talking to 200+ farmers about their drying challenges, losses, and needs. This grounded our design in real-world requirements.

2

Prototype Development & Testing

Our engineering team built 5 prototype iterations, testing different heating methods, airflow designs, and solar configurations. Each was field-tested with farmer feedback.

3

Pilot Program with 20 Farmers

We deployed 20 units across Uganda for a 6-month pilot, collecting data on drying times, losses, quality, and durability. Results exceeded expectations: 45% loss reduction confirmed.

4

Full Launch & Continuous Improvement

Now in full production, we maintain close relationships with farmers, continuously gathering feedback and making improvements. Innovation never stops at Eco Flash Dryer.
